02 — Logo System

The Beaver Pest Defense Logo

The primary logo is a shield badge featuring the Beaver Pest Defense mascot — a fierce beaver head — with "BEAVER" in bold metallic type, "PEST DEFENSE" in brand lime green, and pest silhouettes (spider, ant, cockroach, rodent) across the lower shield panel.

✓ Primary use — dark background (#0A0A0A / #111111)

✓ Approved — charcoal background (#1C1C1C)

Logo Anatomy

Shield Frame

Chrome-to-lime gradient border enclosing the full composition. Never recolor or flatten the shield.

Mascot

Gray/silver beaver head with green eyes. Never swap the mascot or alter fur coloring.

Wordmark

"BEAVER" in metallic white block type. "PEST DEFENSE" in brand lime (#6DC02F). Never change font or split on separate lines differently.

Tagline

"RELENTLESS PROTECTION. GUARANTEED." in small caps below the wordmark. White body, lime period after GUARANTEED.

Pest Icons

Spider, ant, cockroach, rodent silhouettes in chrome/white across the lower panel. May be used independently as service identifiers.

Minimum Size & Clear Space

Minimum digital width: 48px

Minimum print width: 0.5 in

Clear space: equal to 1× the shield height on all sides

Never crowd the logo with text, borders, or competing graphics

✓ Do Use on black (#0A0A0A) or dark charcoal (#111111 – #1C1C1C) backgrounds only
Maintain original proportions — never stretch or squash
Use the full badge as the primary logo mark
Use adequate clear space around the logo at all times
✗ Don't Place the logo on light, white, or medium-gray backgrounds (black bg blends)
Alter colors, recolor the shield, or change font treatments
Add drop shadows, glows, or filters beyond the original
Rotate, skew, or apply perspective transforms
Recreate the logo in CSS or substitute another mascot

Color Usage Rules

  • Brand Lime (#6DC02F) is the only accent color. Do not introduce additional accent hues (red, blue, orange) in brand materials.
  • All backgrounds must fall within the Black–Charcoal–Dark Panel range for brand consistency.
  • Lime on black passes WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 for normal text). Use at 16px+ for text accessibility.
  • The top accent rule is always the full lime-to-lime-dim gradient: linear-gradient(90deg, #6DC02F, #4E8B21 60%, transparent).
  • Never place lime (#6DC02F) text on white backgrounds — contrast fails WCAG AA.

Icon Usage

  • Pest silhouettes: silver (#C8C8C8) or lime (#6DC02F) on dark backgrounds only.
  • Shield mark (lime fill, black check): use as a trust or guarantee badge across all materials.

Section Separator

Lime-to-transparent gradient rule at section starts. Max-width varies by context. Height: 2px.

Layout Principles

  • Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl), horizontal padding 1.5rem.
  • Section vertical padding: 80–112px on desktop, 64px on mobile.
  • No border-radius on any structural elements (buttons, cards, inputs). The brand identity is sharp and industrial.
  • Cards and panels: background #1C1C1C, border 1px solid #2A2A2A, hover border-color shifts to #4E8B21.
  • Alternating section backgrounds: Black (#0A0A0A) ↔ Charcoal (#111111) to create depth without color variance.
  • Ghost/watermark shield: opacity: 0.04–0.05, positioned at section edge to add brand presence without distraction.
07 — Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Beaver Pest Defense speaks with the confidence of a specialist, not the warmth of a neighbor. The audience is business owners and facility managers — people who need results, not reassurance.

We Are

  • Confident and direct ("We eliminate the problem")
  • Professional and discreet (no sensationalism)
  • Outcome-focused ("zero disruption to your operations")
  • Authoritative but not arrogant

We Are Not

  • Casual or folksy (this is a B2B service)
  • Fear-mongering or alarmist about pests
  • Over-selling or using hyperbole
  • Using pest puns or bug humor in formal materials

Writing Rules

  • Headlines: Barlow Condensed, ALL CAPS, bold
  • Body: Sentence case, active voice
  • CTAs: Action verbs — "Get a Quote", "Schedule an Inspection"
  • Avoid: "We're happy to help", "Amazing", "Affordable"

Sample Copy Patterns

  • "Reliable, discreet, and effective."
  • "Pest control that works so you can focus on what matters."
  • "Protecting buildings. Protecting businesses."
  • "Long-term protection. Compliance support. Peace of mind."
08 — What to Avoid

Common Mistakes

✗ Using light or white backgrounds The logo has a black background — placing it on light surfaces creates a black box artifact. All brand materials use dark backgrounds.
✗ Introducing additional accent colors Brand Lime (#6DC02F) is the sole accent. Do not add red, orange, teal, or blue for variety.
✗ Using rounded corners or pill shapes on structural UI The brand is industrial and sharp. No border-radius on buttons, cards, or inputs (small icon containers are the only exception).
✗ Using a different font for headings Barlow Condensed is non-negotiable for all display text. Impact or Arial Narrow are acceptable emergency web fallbacks only.
✗ Placing lime text on white #6DC02F on #FFFFFF fails WCAG AA contrast. Lime text is for dark background use only.
✗ Altering the logo for "dark mode" or "light mode" versions There is no light-mode version of the logo. The site and all materials are dark-only.
